Setwood is a clean and easy-to-use WordPress Blog theme ideal for personal blog, fashion, photography, travel, food etc. or just about anyone who likes to share stories whether it's about life, fashion, travel, or just a simple personal blog.

Setwood theme provides you plenty of useful customization options so you can modify the theme exactly as you want.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the **setwood.zip** folder to the **/wp-content/themes/** directory
2. Activate by going to **Appearance -> Themes**
